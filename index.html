About Me

SQL Developer

Hi, I'm Sreayes Ramanathan from Villupuram. I recently completed my Bachelor's Degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering at Mailam Engineering College. I finished my schooling at AKT Academy Matric Hr Sec School in Kallakuruchi. During my studies, I worked on a project titled "Bus Pay," which integrates digital payments into bus transportation. I also completed an Internship as a "Junior Web Developer" at Hysas Technologies Pvt Ltd, where I developed responsive websites. Additionally, I completed a UX design project and developed a travel app called "HODOPHILEY." I'm proficient in Front-End Technologies such as HTML, CSS, and frameworks like Bootstrap, as well as tools like Figma for UX design. I also have experience with SQL and Java. I was honored with the "Best Outgoing Student of the Batch 2020-2024" award at Mailam Engineering College. I have strong leadership qualities, having served as the representative for my batch from 2020 to 2024. Throughout these four years, I organized and led three industrial visits for my classmates, earning appreciation from my Head of the Department. In addition, I served as the Event Manager for the English Literacy Club at Mailam Engineering College, where I organized various technical events such as paper presentations, quiz competitions, project competitions, and group discussions. My efforts were recognized and appreciated by the principal. My role model is MS Dhoni, from whom I've learned valuable lessons in leadership and how to handle pressure in tough situations. A quote by him that I follow closely is, "Don't focus on the results; focus on the progress." I believe that if you work hard and trust the process, success will follow. That's a bit about me.
